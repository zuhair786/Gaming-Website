IT TAKES TWO

As the name suggests, you’ll need to recruit a friend to play It Takes Two – like Hazelight’s previous game A Way Out, it’s a multiplayer only endeavour. The rom-com-esque story involves a feuding couple who must learn to work together, and the platforming gameplay ingeniously demands the same of you and your player two.

THE MEDIUM

The Medium has psychological horror written all over it. You play as a mortician haunted by a child’s murder, and as you investigate, you enter a surreal spirit world where your real life actions are reproduced simultaneously. Navigating these two planes of existence is key to discovering the fate of the Niwa hotel.

HUMANKIND

Amplitude is making Humankind—a historical 4X game to directly rival Civilization 6. It’s the game the Endless Space and Endless Legend studio always wanted to make, and with Sega’s backing are finally able to realize as a big budget strategy game.
